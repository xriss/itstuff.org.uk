Bonus Content! Leeds Hack 2015 by Brian Ackroyd by {_blog_post_json.author} on 2016-02-03

This special Bonus Content! podcast by our roving reporter, Brian Ackroyd features interviews of hackers and participants at the Leeds Hack 2015 event by Dom Hodgson held at Leeds City Museum.


Thank you very much to everyone who came and participated, especially to the fantastic and ever-funny event organiser, Dom Hodgson and his team of ever-patient and hardworking volunteers without whom none of this would be possible.

We can't thank you enough for creating such a fun and memorable event where techies can come from all corners of the UK (& beyond) to meet and hack together in such unique locations every year!


You can hear Brian's report in IT Stuff Episode 130116 and in upcoming episodes!
