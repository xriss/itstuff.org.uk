History

The IT Stuff radio show came about as a collaboration between members of the Bradford GNU & Linux User Group in 2012.

Since then, we've had a few presenters come and go, a guest who refused to come back, live fact-checking and even a call in during a recording to report on a hackathon.

We've gone through a few changes in our programming format ~ from rather verbose, serious public announcements to all out jokes and fan fare. Ultimately, it all boils down to how any of us was feeling during broadcasting.

You could even say, the current host of presenters are old friends that come together once a month to chat about the current state of the tech industry.
